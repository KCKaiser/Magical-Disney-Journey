The Madlibs portion of the project mainly used python’s natural language processing toolkit, and javascript. Quotes from each character where taken from the internet and loaded into a pandas dataframe. “Stop words,” or common such as ‘the,’ and “it” where removed. Then a human labeled the part of speech and loaded key words into a json file. Javascript/ D3 was used to iterate through the data, randomly pick keywords and deliver the contents to the html page.
